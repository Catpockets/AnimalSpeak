## Mission Statement

Animal Speak was created for my autistic son and for children like him.

The mission of this project is to provide a calm, joyful, accessible animal-learning experience that remains free for children and families who need it.

You are welcome to study it, share it, improve it, adapt it, and use it to help children learn, communicate, and play.

Please do not take this work, repackage it, and sell it back to the families and children it was created to support.
